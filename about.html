Reframing the Global Narrative of Black Art Through Strategy, Scholarship, and Market Intelligence.

Ludlow E. Bailey is a globally recognized cultural curator, art advisor, and market strategist specializing in Contemporary African Diaspora Art.

With a career spanning more than three decades and travel to over 150 countries, Bailey brings a rare combination of curatorial vision, global market intelligence, and executive-level business expertise to the international art world.

Born with roots in Panama and Jamaica and raised between New York and London, his perspective is inherently diasporic—shaped by movement, cross-cultural exchange, and a deep understanding of global systems and cultural identity.
